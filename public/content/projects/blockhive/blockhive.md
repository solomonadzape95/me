## stack
next.js, tailwind v4, tanstack query, radix ui, shadcn, zustand, zod, supabase

## about
the block hive is a community space in nsukka for student builders, early-stage founders, and local blockchain organisers. the marketing site has to prove the space is both practical infrastructure (desks, network, events) and a social engine — without looking like a generic startup landing page or a dark-neon crypto site.

## notes
the design context favours sun-warmed paper tones, deep ink contrast, and amber accents already present in the brand. heavy emphasis on tactile, bulletin-board, residency-journal energy over sleek abstract polish.
